## Meeting Types

Select the type that matches your meeting:

### Daily Standup (15 minutes)
- What I did yesterday
- What I'll do today
- Blockers I need help with

### Weekly Team Sync (30-60 minutes)
- Progress on goals
- Blockers and dependencies
- Upcoming work
- Process improvements

### Retrospective (60-90 minutes)
- What went well
- What didn't go well
- Action items for improvement
- Celebrate wins

### Planning Session (60-90 minutes)
- Review goals and requirements
- Decompose into tickets
- Estimate effort
- Assign ownership

### Incident Post-Mortem (60 minutes)
- Timeline of events
- Root cause analysis
- Action items for prevention
- Lessons learned

### 1:1 Check-in (30 minutes)
- How are you doing?
- Progress and blockers
- Growth and development
- Feedback
